Performance Management



HR departments play a crucial role in employee performance management. They help establish performance goals and conduct regular performance reviews. HR professionals provide feedback, support, and guidance to employees to help them improve their performance and contribute effectively to the organization.

Employee Benefit Administration


HR is responsible for managing employee benefits programs, such as health insurance, retirement plans, and other perks. They help employees enroll in these programs, address benefits-related inquiries, and ensure compliance with relevant regulations.

Recruitment and Staffing



HR departments often manage the recruitment and staffing processes. This includes creating job postings, screening resumes, conducting interviews, and selecting suitable candidates for open positions. They also handle onboarding and orientation for new employees.
